## General channels
- `#engineering-all` Broadly for all engineers (internal and external) to share about engineering updates, questions, and more. Our most-used channel
- `#engineering-hot-takes` Our fun channel for posting your engineering-related hot takes. No NJ-project-specific hot takes please
- `#engineering-pairing-is-caring` For coordinating Friday cross-team pairing sessions
- `#accessibility-all` Not engineering-specific, but recommend everyone join, Go-to place for a11y questions and support 

## Initiative channels
- `#resx-eng` Team channel for ResX initiative engineers
- `#business-dev` Team channel for BizX initiative engineers

## OOI internal channels
- `#engineering-internal` Limited to OOI engineers only. For conversations that are specifically internal.
- `#resx-eng-internal` OOI engineers only, for ResX internal conversations
- `#business-eng-internal` OOI engineers only, for BizX internal conversations
